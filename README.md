# track2gether-backend

## Project Description

Track2gether, a budget tracker, is an application that helps to keep an accurate record of your money inflow and outflow. 
It allows you to monitor and categorize your income sources and expenses. 
Without the help of a fancy financial planner, we provide an easy and light way to monitor and track your income and expenses for you to have a better idea of you and your partnerâ€™s financial status by providing visualized data on financial history.
